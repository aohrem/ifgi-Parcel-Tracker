The ifgi Parcel Tracker is a tracking system which allows you to keep your parcel's condition in sight. This Website can be used to register new parcles and to display the sensor data of parcels which are being shipped right now. The ifgi Parcel Tracker measures location-, temperature- and humidity and notices if the parcel crashes or is opened on it's way.
